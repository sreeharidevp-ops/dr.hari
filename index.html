Heritage & Legacy

800 Years of Healing Tradition

I come from a Kerala family where my father's lineage has practiced Ayurveda for over 800 years, with deep expertise in Cancer, Mental Disorders, and Kidney Disorders. My mother's family brings centuries of knowledge in Agatatantra (toxicology), a rare branch of traditional medicine.

In 2010, I relocated to Karnataka's Dakshina Kannada district to be near the Western Ghats, where our pharmacy sources rare wild herbs with the help of local villagers — herbs that are essential to the efficacy and purity of our medicines.

Our Pharmacy & Medicine Philosophy

Our medicines are 100% pure herbs, prepared using the traditional Suvarna Udweepani technique — no bhasma, no heavy metals, no synthetic chemicals or preservatives. Every preparation follows the ancestral methods refined over generations of Arbuta Chikilsa (cancer treatment).

What We Treat

Our Areas of Speciality

Combining ancestral herbal knowledge with integrative medicine, we offer proven treatment pathways for complex, chronic conditions.

Cancer Treatment (Arbuta Chikilsa)

A generations-old tradition of cancer care using the Suvarna Udweepani technique — pure herbal preparations with no heavy metals. We support modern treatment by reducing chemotherapy & radiation side effects, boosting immunity, and preventing relapse.

Parkinson's Disease (Kamba Vata)

Our unique multi-generational herbal formula aims to prevent neuron damage in the substantia nigra, reduce oxidative stress in mitochondria, and enhance natural dopamine secretion — helping patients reduce reliance on Levodopa over time.

Kidney Disorders

Treating kidney stones, CKD, diabetic nephropathy, nephritis, and nephrotic syndromes. Our structured 105-day treatment course with test-dose monitoring (21, 45 days) ensures safety and measurable creatinine, urea, and electrolyte improvements.

Dementia & Neurological Disorders

Drawing on ancestral knowledge in mental disorders, we support patients with memory decline and neurological conditions using traditional neuroprotective herbs tailored to the individual's condition and stage.

Integrative & Preventive Care

We adopt an integrative approach — traditional treatments run parallel to modern medicine. Prevention of relapse, immunity enhancement, and holistic recovery are core pillars of every patient's plan.

Agatatantra (Toxicology)

Rooted in centuries of expertise from my mother's family, Agatatantra addresses toxic conditions in the body using traditional detoxification methods and rare wild herb formulations sourced from the Western Ghats.
