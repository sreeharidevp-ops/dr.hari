Heritage & Legacy

800 Years of Ayurvedic Healing Tradition in Kerala

I come from a Kerala family where the father's lineage has practised Ayurvedic medicine for over 800 years, with deep expertise in Cancer (Arbuda Chikilsa), Mental & Neurological Disorders, and Kidney Disease Management. My mother's family brings centuries of knowledge in Agadatantra (Ayurvedic Toxicology) — a rare and highly specialised branch of traditional medicine.

Since 2010, rare medicinal herbs have been ethically sourced from the Western Ghats to ensure purity, potency, and authenticity in every formulation.

Our Herbal Medicine Philosophy

Our medicines are herbal formulations with no heavy metals, no synthetic chemicals, and no preservatives. Prepared using the traditional Suvarna Udweepani technique, every preparation follows multi-generational ancestral formulas refined over centuries of practice.
